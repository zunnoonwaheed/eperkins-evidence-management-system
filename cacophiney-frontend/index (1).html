What We Resolve

Every tax problem has a relief path.

The IRS offers more resolution programs than most taxpayers ever hear about. We match your situation to the right one and manage the entire process.

⚖

Offer in Compromise

Settle eligible tax debt for less than the full balance when full payment would create financial hardship.

⏹

Garnishment & Levy Release

Stop active wage garnishments and bank levies, and prevent new enforcement while your case is worked.

✎

Penalty Abatement

Remove or reduce failure-to-file and failure-to-pay penalties that inflate your balance year after year.

📅

Installment Agreements

Structured monthly payment plans negotiated at amounts your budget can actually sustain.

📄

Unfiled Returns

Get back into filing compliance — often the required first step before any relief program applies.

🏛

State Tax Issues

State back taxes, liens, and payment plans handled alongside your federal case in all 50 states.
